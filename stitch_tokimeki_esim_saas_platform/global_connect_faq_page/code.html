Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install my eSIM? expand_more
Most devices allow installation by scanning the QR code sent to your email. Go to Settings > Cellular > Add eSIM. For manual installation, use the SM-DP+ Address and Activation Code provided in your Global Connect dashboard.
Is my device compatible? expand_more
Global Connect works with iPhone XS and newer, Google Pixel 3 and newer, and most flagship Samsung devices since 2020. Your device must also be carrier-unlocked to use international eSIMs.
When should I activate my data plan? expand_more
We recommend installing the eSIM before you fly, but only activating the plan (turning on Data Roaming) once you land at your destination. The validity period typically begins when the eSIM connects to a supported network.
Can I keep my WhatsApp number? expand_more
Yes! Your eSIM only provides data. You can keep your primary SIM active for calls and SMS, and your WhatsApp will remain linked to your original phone number without any interruption.
How do I top up my data? expand_more
Simply log into your Global Connect account, select your active plan, and click 'Top-up'. You can add more GBs instantly without having to reinstall a new eSIM profile.
