Pro Tip

Dial *#06# on your phone. If you see an EID number, your device is eSIM compatible.

How to activate your eSIM

ios iOS Activation

Fast Setup
1

Go to Settings

Open 'Cellular' or 'Mobile Data' on your iPhone.

2

Add eSIM

Tap 'Add eSIM' or 'Add Cellular Plan' to begin.

3

Scan QR Code

Scan the QR code provided in your Global Connect confirmation email.

android Android Activation

1

Network Settings

Navigate to Settings > Network & Internet > SIMs.

2

Download SIM

Tap '+' and select 'Download a SIM instead?'.

3

Confirm Activation

Scan your QR code and follow the on-screen prompts to activate.
